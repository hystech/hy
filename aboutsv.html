About Us

SAVMOR ENGINEERING PRIVATE LIMITED

Savmor Engineering is a leading service provider of automation and control
industry in Singapore that enables customers to improve operating safety,
reliability, and efficiency. Founded in 2011, Savmor is lead by her founded
Steven Lim who is equipped with project management, service maintenance
and engineering know how and their best practices.

The future is bright for Savmor. We are healthy and growing, a testament to
the confidence our clients and employees have in us. With environmental
sustainability on front stage, combined with an increasingly resource
constrained world, our technical and management services will find niche
way that will grow in demand. I look forward to exciting times ahead!

We also offer our clients flexibility with our resources in order to
execute their project in a manner that will that ensure success and safety.

Our mission is to be one of the leading service providers and supports
in Automation and Control with solution back by the highest quality. We
hope to utilize our expertise in the arena of automation technology for
better work, life, and environment.
